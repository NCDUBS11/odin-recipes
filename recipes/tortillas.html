Homemade Flour Tortillas

Description

Store-bought tortillas can't compete with this homemade recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4cups Flour
  • 2tsp Baking Powder
  • 1tsp Salt
  • 2tbsp Lard
  • 1.5cups Water

Steps

  1. Whisk flour, baking powder and salt together in mixing bowl. Rub in lard with fingers until mixture resembles cornmeal. Add water and mix until dough comes together.
  2. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface. Knead until smooth.
  3. Preheat large skillet over medium-high heat. Roll dough into tortilla and place into skillet. Allow to bubble and brown and then flip tortilla to other side. Remove tortilla once browned and place in warmer until serving.
