Carne Asada Tacos

Description

Easy and flavorful. You will love these tacos!

Ingredients

  • 1.5lb top sirloin (or similar), cut to small pieces
  • 0.5tsp salt
  • 1tsp black pepper
  • 1 lime
  • 1 can tomatillos
  • 2 jalapenos, seeded
  • 4Tbsp canola oil
  • 1 can beef broth
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 0.5 large onion
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Place meat into shallow bowl and season with sal, black pepper and red pepper. Squeeze lime juice over meat until coated. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Blend tomatillo and jalapeno until thick. Heat 1tbsp oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Pour in tomatillo mixture. Cook, stirring frequently, for 5 minutes. Add beef broth. Reduce heat and simmer 20 to 30 minutes until mixture coats a spoon. Transfer to serving dish.
  3. Heat 1tbsp of oil in large skillet over high heat. Add beef and saute. Meanwhile, heat tortillas.
  4. Serve with fresh ingredients and tomatillo mixture over tortillas. Garnish with lemon.
